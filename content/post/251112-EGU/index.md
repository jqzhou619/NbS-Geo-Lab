Welcome domestic and foreign experts, scholars, teachers and students to pay attention to this special topic and actively submit papers or participate in the conference for communication and exchange.

<!--more-->

The European Geosciences Union (EGU) conference is one of the most influential annual events in the global field of earth and environmental sciences. It brings together researchers, engineers, and policy practitioners from various disciplines to exchange and collaborate on the cutting-edge developments, methods, and applications in earth system science. The 2026 EGU conference will be held simultaneously in Vienna, Austria (in person) and online (via a web conference) from May 3rd to 8th, 2026. 


Researcher Anni from Zhejiang University, as the primary convenor, jointly organized the special session ITS1.1/NH13.4 "Artificial Intelligence Enhancing the Resilience of Hydrogeological Disasters under the Background of Climate Change： Modeling， Assessment and Decision Support" with Professor Yangzi Qiu from the National University of Bridges and Roads in France and Professor Xiaogang Shi from the University of Glasgow in the UK.
